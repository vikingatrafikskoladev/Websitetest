About Us

At Vikinga Traffic School, we’re more than just a driving school — we’re your dedicated partner on the road to becoming a confident and responsible driver. Based in Södertälje, we may be newly established, but our team brings years of teaching experience, a strong local reputation, and a passion for quality instruction.

We’ve listened carefully to our students’ needs and created a flexible, affordable training model that fits today's realities. Whether you're just starting or refining your skills, our lessons are tailored to suit all learning styles and budgets.

To make your journey easier, we even offer pickup from all train stations in Södertälje. We believe learning to drive should be accessible, supportive, and stress-free.

Ready to start your journey? Get in touch with us at Vikinga Traffic School — we’re here to help you succeed behind the wheel.
